Activity 3.0: Project - Autonomous Agent with Search

How to play

In our world, there is an agent and a piece of food. You can choose between five different search algorithms to help it find the best path towards its meal.

If no food is found, another piece appear somewhere else on the map.

It is also possible to edit the scenario as you desire . After editing, just press and enjoy what you changed.
